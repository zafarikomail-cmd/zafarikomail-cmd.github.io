About Me

From broadcast media in Kabul to full-stack development in Islamabad — an unconventional path with a clear direction.

I also founded and run the Afghan Student Community at NUTECH entirely from scratch, and represented Afghanistan at NUST Global Village 2026 — recognized by NIC for outstanding contribution.
